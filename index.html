UVA Multicultural Greek Council

The Multicultural Greek Council at the University of Virginia was founded in spring 1999 in order to unify and connect Greek organizations that promoted culural values. The founding members consisted of Omega Phi Beta Sorority, Incorporated, alpha Kappa Delta Phi Sorority, Incorporated, and La Unidad Latina, Lambda Upsilon Lambda Fraternity, Incorporated.
