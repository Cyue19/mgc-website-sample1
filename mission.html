Mission Statement

The purpose of this organization shall be to further the best interests of the fraternities and sororities of the Multicultural Greek Council through representation in both the University and Charlottesville communities, as well as through an active interest in matters of common concern. The Multicultural Greek Council shall concentrate on unifying and creating opportunities for its member organizations.
